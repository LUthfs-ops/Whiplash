The Dark Knight (2008)

Director: Christopher Nolan
★ 9.0/10

Cast: Christian Bale, Heath Ledger, Aaron Eckhart

When the menace known as the Joker wreaks havoc and chaos on the people of Gotham, Batman must accept one of the greatest psychological and physical tests of his ability to fight injustice. The Dark Knight follows Bruce Wayne's Batman as he sets out to dismantle the remaining criminal organizations that plague Gotham City streets.
