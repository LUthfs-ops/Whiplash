The Shawshank Redemption (1994)

Director: Frank Darabont
★ 9.3/10

Cast: Tim Robbins, Morgan Freeman

Over the course of several years, two convicts form a friendship, seeking consolation and, eventually, redemption through basic compassion. Andy Dufresne, a banker wrongly convicted of murder, and Red, the prison contraband smuggler, find hope and perseverance in their harsh environment.
